# Code of Conduct

Akrynix is committed to providing a professional, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors.

---

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:
- Be respectful and constructive
- Accept feedback professionally
- Focus on technical merit, not personal opinions

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Abusive language or personal attacks
- Deliberate disruption of workflows

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may take appropriate action for violations, including removal from the organization.

---  
  
This Code of Conduct applies to all Akrynix repositories and communication channels.
